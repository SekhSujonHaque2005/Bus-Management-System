Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a seat?
Simply log into your student dashboard, view the available active routes, and click "Book Seat". You'll get an instant confirmation.
Is live tracking accurate?
Yes! Our drivers use GPS broadcasting directly from their dashboard, updating the live map every few seconds.
What happens if a bus is full?
The system will notify you that no seats are available and suggest the next available bus on that route.
